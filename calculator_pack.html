Simple Calculator Pack

Welcome! This free calculator pack includes three essential financial tools: calculate your compound interest growth, determine mortgage payments, and plan your savings goals. Perfect for personal finance planning, budgeting, and investment calculations.

1. Compound Interest Calculator

Use this calculator to see how your investments grow over time with monthly contributions and compounding interest. Ideal for retirement planning or wealth growth estimates.

2. Mortgage Loan Payment Calculator

Calculate your monthly mortgage payments easily. Enter your loan amount, interest rate, and term to see your monthly payment, total paid, and interest over the life of the loan.

3. Savings Goal Calculator

Planning for a future goal? Enter your target amount, current savings, expected annual return, and years to save to find out how much you need to save each month to reach your goal.
